Brew Creek Center

Scope

  • - To supply and install LAN and WLAN services for Brew Creek Center.
  • - Create networks for Operations, Staff, Guests and, Corporate.
  • - Provide consultation services to facilitate an efficient Fibre Build Out with Telus.
  • - Connect Network to future Fibre Line and ensure best practices to utilize the full bandwidth
  • - Document and label network equipment
  • - Provide ongoing network monitoring
  • - Install Firewall and Intruder Protection Services

Project Details

The backbone network system is from Ubiquity Systems. Ubiquity has a low base price, excellent quality, and, there are no annual license fees. They are very proactive with firmware updates and support. The equipment comes with a one year warranty. Network notifications and accounts are available for key staff members, and Control Alt Delete provides free monitoring as well.

The Gathering

  • Unifi AP HD
  • UniFi® Security Gateway Pro 4

    • - LiteBeam AC AP

"The Gathering" will serve as the main center for the network. Equipment will be wall-mounted on a small server rack and will have the flexibility to build out for future considerations. The network rack will also support any network requirements in "The Gathering."

Fiber will be run into the building and terminated into the ONT(Optical Network Terminal). From the ONT it is patched into the Gateway(router). The router manages all internal devices and provides the firewall/security. The router is then attached to the 16 port POE(power over Ethernet) switch which will provide power and network connections to the Point to Point radios.

There will be 3-4 radios that will provide a signal to each of the 4 Zones. Each building has a receiver and a WIFI Access point as a minimum. Larger and high use buildings may have extra requirements as detailed below.
